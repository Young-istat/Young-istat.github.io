Young-ISA Twitter Poster Conference (#YISAPoster2022)

Date: October 5-6th, 2022
Location: Twitter - @Young_Stats_ISA
Time: 12.00pm WET Wednesday, October 5th 2022 - 12.00pm WET Thursday, October 6th 2022

The #YISAPoster2022 Twitter Poster Conference is an online conference in Statistics and Data Science organized by Young-ISA and promoted by the Irish Statistical Association.

The event is held entirely on Twitter and aims to provide an opportunity for Statisticians and Data Scientists around the world to meet online, share their research and engage in scientific discussion.

Participants can share their work by tweeting a research poster or infographic using the hashtag #YISAPoster2022 and engage and discuss with others’ by commenting, retweeting and liking.


Join the #YISAPoster2020 Conference:

  • Share a poster or infographic concerning your research in any Statistics and Data Science related topic and elicit reactions and discuss with the community.

  • Tweet your poster with a title, the hashtag #YISAPoster2022 and any other relevant keyword-hashtags beginning at 12:00pm WET on October 5th 2022.

  • Engage and discuss with the other participants throughout the conference by commenting on their #YISAPoster2022 posters, as well as answering their questions on your poster. Promote others’ research via re-tweets and likes.
