Young-ISA Twitter Poster Conference (#YISAPoster2020)

Date: July 15-16th, 2020
Location: Twitter
Time: 12.00pm WET Wednesday, July 15th 2020 - 12.00pm WET Thursday, July 16th 2020

The #YISAPoster2020 Twitter Conference will be held entirely online over Twitter and aims to provide an opportunity for Statistician and Data Scientist around the world to meet online, share thier research and engage in scientific debate.

Join the #YISAPoster2020 event:

  • Register: to submit a poster please register here. The closing date is July 13th 2020.

  • Tweet your poster with a title, #YISAPoster2020 and any other relevant hashtag(s) – during the 24h conference beginning 12:00pm WET July 15th 2020.

  • Discuss and engage, throughout the 24h conference, with other researchers by commenting on their #YISAPoster2020 posters as well as making sure to answer questions from the committee and other researchers on your #YISAPoster2020 poster.

  • Prizes will be given to people whose #YISAPoster2020 posters are deemed best by our committee and the one who get more retweets/likes.
